Gaussian Splatting for Digital Twins
3D Assets Generation From Images

Abstract

This website serves a showcase for the Gaussian Splatting technique applied to the creation of 3D models from images. The technique is based on the idea of splatting points in a 3D space. The points are gathered from images using Structure From Motion (SfM) algorithms (such as COLMAP). The result is a 3D model that can be viewed from different angles.
